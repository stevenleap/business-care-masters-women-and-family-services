Field Notes

Care Masters Women and Family Services is a nonprofit based in Mississauga, Ontario, dedicated to promoting health, relieving poverty, and supporting at-risk youth. We offer counselling, food assistance, and mentorship programs to help women, families, and young people build stronger futures.

Our work is rooted in the community: every session, every meal, every workshop is a step toward lasting strength.

Support Paths

Counselling & Support Groups

Health promotion through counselling and support groups for at-risk women and families.

Food & Necessities Program

Relieving poverty by providing food and basic necessities to individuals and families in need.

Youth Workshops & Recreation

Addressing youth challenges through counselling workshops and healthy recreational opportunities.

Mentorship & Leadership

Developing mentoring and business leadership programs for youth and women.
